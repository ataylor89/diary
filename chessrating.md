# chess rating

I have a very high chess rating on chess.com

I know the truth about chess rating

Chess rating is highly connected to time and money

I have been poor my whole life... how do I have a high chess rating?

I have worked 9 to 5 day jobs since graduating college... how do I have a high chess rating?

Honestly, I have spent a lot of time studying chess

I also have spent a lot of time playing chess

I use a combination of techniques to study chess

I read chess books, I take Chessable courses, I take lessons on chess.com (especially Jeremy Silman's lessons)

I play puzzle rush on chess.com (I have gotten a 40 on the 5 minute puzzle rush feature)

I use the tactics trainer on chess.com (I have gotten a 3300 or a 3400 rating on the tactics trainer)

I watch chess streamers on YouTube, Kick, and Twitch

I read Mikhail Tal's My Life and Games in high school

I read Yasser Seirawan's Winning Chess Tactics in high school

I read Bobby Fischer's My 60 Memorable Games in high school

I read Bruce Pandolfini's Endgame Workshop in college

I took Anish Giri's Chessable course on the Sicilian dragon in 2022 or 2023

I read Yasser Seirawan's book Winning Chess Strategies in my thirties

I play through World Championship games, like the games by Magnus Carlsen, Vishy Anand, Vladimir Kramnik, Garry Kasparov, Anatoly Karpov, and Bobby Fischer

I read Vladimir Kramnik's book My Life and Games in college

I watch chess streamers like Daniel Naroditsky, Levy Rozman, and Hikaru Nakamura

I watch Magnus Carlsen when he streams

I learned how to play the endgame by studying Magnus Carlsen's games

I learned how to play the endgame by reading Bruce Pandolfini's book Endgame Workshop

I learned a lot about tactics by reading Mikhail Tal's My Life and Games

So listen

Chess rating doesn't matter

What matters is your enjoyment of the game

I have put so many hours into studying chess

Chess rating is connected to time and money

People who are wealthy, people who have a lot of free time, have an advantage in competitive chess

But it's also the case that... you have to spend a lot of time on chess to improve your rating

I have spent a lot of time playing chess and studying chess
