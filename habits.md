# habits

My family taught me the following habits:

1. Be vegan
2. Stay home (all the time)
3. Never have sex (not before marriage, not after marriage)

We can evaluate each item of advice

Be vegan is obviously the way

(In a book called The Analects, the students of Confucius describe good behavior as "the way")

Stay home (all the time) is a habit that minimizes risk and maximizes safety

It's important to put safety first

So I stay home

Never have sex is obviously the way

It's part of being vegan

If you want to be vegan, eat a vegan diet, and never have sex

If you had sex in the past, don't worry about it

It's really the case that... sex is not safe

So best safety advice is to never have sex

Best safety advice is to never have sex... not before marriage, not after marriage

We have finished analyzing the three items on our list

Each item of advice is sensible, important, and worth sharing

That's why I wrote this document... because I think that the list is worth sharing
