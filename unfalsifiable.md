# unfalsifiable

When we read a statement, it can be true, false, possibly true, or unfalsifiable

For example...

Statement 1: The US government is evil\
Analysis: This statement is unfalsifiable

Statement 2: The US government is a mix of good and evil\
Analysis: Honestly, it's a true statement

Statement 3: The US military is evil\
Analysis: This statement is unfalsifiable

Statement 4: There are many good men in the US military\
Analysis: This is a true statement

Statement 5: There are many evil men in the US military\ 
Analysis: This is a true statement

Statement 6: There is an evil gang in the US military\
Analysis: This is a true statement

You can see that statements 1 and 3 are unfalsifiable

Statement 2 is reasonable and it's the truth

Statement 4 is important because it protects every good man in the US military

Statement 5 is necessary because it's life saving information

Statement 6 is necessary because it explains a lot and it's life saving information

So you see...
