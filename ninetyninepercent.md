# 99 percent

I have said on my Github, "99% of men in America are untrustworthy"

I have also said, "99% of men in America are not competent at math"

You might ask, "How do you feel comfortable making these statements?"

I actually have a proof for each of these statements

They are sacred poems

I protect all sacred writing

There are many women, in many parts of the world, who have written these poems

There are many men, in many parts of the world, who have written these poems

So when I say, "99% of men in America are untrustworthy," I have a proof for this statement

When I say, "99% of men in America are not competent at math," I have a proof for this statement

The proof is that they are sacred poems

Not everything is a sacred poem

What gets called a sacred poem?

Well I think that a sacred poem is a poem by a sacred author

Honestly, that's my understanding of the phrase "sacred poetry"

Later on I might acquire other definitions of the phrase "sacred poetry", and add them to my Github

I wanted to share the reasoning behind these statements

They are sacred poems, and that is why I feel comfortable writing them down
